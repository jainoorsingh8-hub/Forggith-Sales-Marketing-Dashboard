# Forggith Sales & Marketing Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive Power BI dashboard developed for Forggith Pharmaceuticals, a pharmaceutical manufacturing company based in Germany. The dashboard provides actionable insights into sales and marketing performance, enabling management, sales teams, and executives to monitor KPIs and make data-driven decisions.

## Business Problem
Forggith Pharmaceuticals sells its products through distributors and requires a reporting solution to:

* Monitor sales performance across regions and channels.
* Track revenue against business targets.
* Analyze product performance.
* Evaluate marketing effectiveness.
* Support strategic decision-making through KPI monitoring.

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Sales Performance Overview

Key Metrics:

* Total Revenue
* Revenue YTD
* Previous Year YTD Revenue
* Same Period Last Year Revenue
* Total Target
* Target YTD
* Revenue Performance YTD vs Target YTD
* Revenue Month-on-Month Growth %

Visualizations:

* Revenue Trend Analysis
* Revenue by Location
* Revenue by Channel
* Revenue by Product Class
* Revenue by Distributor
* Revenue vs Target Comparison

### 2. Marketing Performance

Key Metrics:

* Revenue Achieved vs Revenue Target
* Volume Achieved vs Volume Target
* Revenue Achievement %
* Volume Achievement %

Visualizations:

* Team Performance Analysis
* Product Performance Analysis
* Target Achievement Tracking
* Revenue and Volume Analysis

## Data Model

The project follows a Star Schema model consisting of:

* FactSales
* FactTargets
* DimProducts
* DimEmployees
* DimLocation
* DimChannel
* DimSubChannel
* Calendar Table
